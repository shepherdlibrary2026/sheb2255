Theme

Recovering spiritual power by returning to biblical devotion, fellowship, prayer, generosity, and Spirit-led evangelism.

Key Scriptures

  • Acts 2:42-47 — The devotion and growth of the early church
  • Acts 4:31-35 — Boldness, unity, and generosity
  • Ephesians 4:11-16 — Equipping the saints for ministry

Major Teaching Points

  • The early church was devoted to doctrine, prayer, fellowship, and breaking of bread.
  • Spiritual vitality is cultivated through obedience, unity, and Holy Spirit empowerment.
  • Every believer is called to serve with their spiritual gifts for the growth of the body.

Discussion Questions

  • What priorities made the early church spiritually powerful?
  • What has the modern church gained, and what has it lost?
  • How can our local church cultivate deeper fellowship and spiritual growth?
